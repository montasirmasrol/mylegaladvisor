Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a lawyer?

Find a lawyer from the list, open their profile, and submit an appointment request with your preferred date and time. You must be logged in as a user to book.

What types of lawyers are available?

Criminal, Civil, Family, Corporate, Immigration, Tax, Employment, Real Estate, Personal Injury, and more.

How do ratings work?

Users can rate lawyers from 1-5 stars and leave optional comments after logging in.

Do I need an account to see lawyers?

No. You can browse lawyers and view their details without logging in. You only need an account to book and rate.
